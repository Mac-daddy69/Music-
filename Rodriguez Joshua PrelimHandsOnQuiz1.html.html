Cubao

Location

Araneta City, formerly known as Araneta Center, is a bustling commercial hub in Cubao, Quezon City, Philippines. This vibrant district is known for its shopping malls, entertainment venues, and business centers. With its strategic location and accessibility, it serves as a central point for commuters, shoppers, and event-goers. Whether you're here for shopping, food, or concerts, Araneta City has something for everyone.

History

Araneta City was founded in 1952 by J. Amado Araneta, a visionary businessman who sought to create a world-class commercial district. Over the years, it has transformed into a premier lifestyle and business destination. One of its most iconic landmarks, the Araneta Coliseum, opened in 1960 and became internationally renowned for hosting significant events, including the legendary "Thrilla in Manila" boxing match between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier in 1975. Today, Araneta City continues to grow, blending modern infrastructure with its rich historical legacy.
